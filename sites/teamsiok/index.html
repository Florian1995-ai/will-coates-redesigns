Brad Siok– Your Trusted State Farm Insurance Agent in Peoria, AZ

Hello Arizona! Our team is excited to meet you! I’m Brad, a proud State Farm® Insurance Agent located in Peoria, AZ. _______________________________________________________________________

Local Insurance for Arizona Families & Businesses

If you’re looking for a reliable insurance agent in the Phoenix Metro Area—including Peoria, Glendale, Surprise, and Scottsdale—our team is ready to serve you! We proudly assist customers across Arizona, California, Colorado, and Nevada, whether you’re a longtime resident or relocating to the area.

Peoria and the surrounding communities are home to families, entrepreneurs, and outdoor enthusiasts who deserve coverage that fits their unique needs—from protecting homes in the desert foothills to covering vehicles for daily commutes through the Valley.

With over 260 combined years of insurance experience, our dedicated team brings both local insight and State Farm’s trusted protection to every relationship. We’re proud to have earned multiple top honors, including the President’s Club Trophy—recognizing our agency as one of the top 10 State Farm agencies nationwide for six consecutive years.

About Brad

I started my State Farm career over 31 years ago as a homeowners claims adjuster, traveling the country to help customers recover from losses. That experience still guides me every day as I assist our customers through their own claims.

As a second-generation State Farm Agent, I grew up watching my father run his successful agency in Schaumburg, IL (retired after nearly 50 years). I followed in his footsteps, opening my own agency in Peoria in 1999.

I’m a Chicago native, a 1994 graduate of Purdue University, and a lifelong learner who has earned the CPCU®, CLU®, ChFC®, and CASL® designations. I’m also a proud graduate of Tony Robbins’ UPW and Business Mastery programs—always striving to grow as a leader and business owner.
